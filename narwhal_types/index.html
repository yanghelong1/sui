    PreSubscribedBroadcastSender is a wrapped Broadcast channel that limits subscription to initialization time. This is designed to be used for cancellation signal to all the components, and the limitation is intended to prevent a component missing the shutdown signal due to a subscription that happens after the shutdown signal was sent. The receivers have a special peek method which can be used to conditionally check for shutdown signal on the channel.
